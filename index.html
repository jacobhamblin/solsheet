Momentum Tracker
Core Class Feature
Combat Momentum Core · d10 HD · STR & CON saves

Max MP: Proficiency Bonus + CON modifier (min 1) · Resets at end of combat or 1 min without hostile activity

Gaining MP: Hit a creature — max 1/turn · Take damage — max 1/turn · Reduce creature to 0 HP — gain 2 MP

Spending: Once per attack and once per non-attack action on your turn

Momentum Options:

  • 1 MP — Quick Reload: Reload or swap weapons without using an action
  • 2 MP — Push Through: Move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks
  • 2 MP — Precision Burst: +2 to hit, or bonus damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus
  • 3 MP — Suppression: On hit, target has disadvantage on its next attack roll before end of its next turn
  • 4 MP — Overdrive: Gain one extra bonus action this turn (reload, swap, Brace, or activate a Marine feature)

Infiltrator
Silent Kill & Ghost Entry Level 3

Silent Kill: When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack while hidden, invisible, or unseen, and the target is unaware of you, deal additional damage equal to twice your Precision Strike dice. Non-elite targets (GM discretion) must also make a CON save or drop to 0 HP. Once per turn.

Ghost Entry: Advantage on checks to infiltrate guarded areas quietly, bypass doors/vents/hatches without detection, and conceal signs of entry or bodies.

Vanish Strike Level 7

When you deal Precision Strike damage with a melee attack, move up to half your speed without provoking OAs. If you end in cover, darkness, or light obscurement, you may attempt to Hide. If the target drops to 0 HP, creatures that didn't directly witness the strike don't auto-detect you.

Predator Veil Level 10

May attempt to Hide while lightly obscured, in cluttered interiors, or in dense foot traffic. Bonus action: Shroud yourself in kinetic camouflage for 1 turn — effectively invisible. Ends immediately after you attack, force a save, or are clearly revealed. If Silent Kill is used while Predator Veil is active, target has disadvantage on the CON save.

/ long rest = Proficiency Bonus
Erase Target Level 15

Once per turn, when you hit with a melee attack while hidden/invisible/target totally unaware: maximum weapon damage · maximum Precision Strike damage · target makes CON save. Failure: non-elite → drops to 0 HP; others take damage and can't take reactions. Success: normal damage · can't speak or activate vocal alarms until end of its next turn.

/ long rest = Proficiency Bonus
Saboteur
Explosive Expert & Trap Master Level 3

Explosive Expert: Explosives (grenades, charges, mines, planted traps) deal bonus damage = PB.

Trap Master: Set a number of traps = PB per long rest. Set Trap (action): Place in unoccupied space within 5 ft. Trap Detection/Disarm DC = 8 + PB + INT mod. When triggered: DEX save (Specialist DC) — failure: 2d8 damage + speed 0 until end of next turn; success: half, no movement penalty. Active traps at once = INT modifier (min 1).

Prescient Timing Level 7
  • Creatures make DEX saves with disadvantage against your explosives and traps
  • Creatures that fail the save cannot take reactions until end of their next turn
Chain Detonation Level 10

Once per turn, when one of your explosives or traps deals damage, trigger a secondary detonation. Secondary blast originates from the same point, affects creatures within 10 ft, deals half the original damage. Creatures make a DEX save (Specialist DC) — failure: full secondary damage; success: half.

Cataclysm Protocol Level 15

Once per short or long rest, when an explosive or trap deals damage, activate Cataclysm Protocol: maximum possible damage · ignores armor and damage reduction · automatically triggers Chain Detonation without its once-per-turn limit. Saving throws still apply normally.

/ short or long rest
Marksman
Deadeye Optics & Patient Aim Level 3

Deadeye Optics: First ranged attack on your turn against a target beyond Close range gains +2 to hit. If made from cover or while hidden, first hit each turn also deals additional damage = WIS or INT modifier (chosen at L3).

Patient Aim: If you move no more than half your speed on your turn, your next ranged attack before end of turn: ignores half and three-quarters cover · reroll one damage die · if it misses, your exact position isn't revealed unless clearly observed.

Terrain Ghost Level 7

While in cover, lightly obscured, on elevated terrain, or firing through broken line of sight: ranged attacks against you are made with disadvantage (until you move into open space, are clearly revealed, or become incapacitated). Once on each of your turns after hitting with a ranged attack, move up to 10 ft without provoking OAs (must end in cover or a valid firing position if possible).

Kill Zone Level 10

Bonus action — Choose one creature you can see. Until start of your next turn, the first ranged weapon attack that hits it: crit range 19–20 · +2d8 damage · ignores half and three-quarters cover · target's movement −10 ft until end of its next turn. If already under a movement penalty when hit, target also cannot take reactions.

Unseen Reaper Level 15

If hidden when making a ranged attack, your position is not automatically revealed if: weapon is suppressed · attack is at Long range or farther · firing from heavy cover or broken line of sight. After the attack, immediately make a Stealth check to remain hidden. If the attack also applies Precision Strike: add one additional Precision Strike die · target cannot take reactions until end of its next turn.

  • Ghost Movement: Ignore difficult terrain · move through enemy spaces · if you move at least 10 ft before attacking, gain advantage on your next attack that turn

  • Combat Reload: Reloading is a free action once per turn · after reloading, +1 to hit on your next attack before end of the turn
